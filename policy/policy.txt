Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16/10/2024

We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your data. This privacy policy explains how our app collects and uses information.

1. Data We Collect
When you use our app, we collect the following information:

IP Address: Logged automatically when you visit our app.
Geolocation: Collected only if you grant permission for location access.
Datetime of visit: Logged automatically when you visit our app.

2. How We Use Your Data
We log the collected IP addresses and geolocation data (we log geolocated data only if user permission is granted) mainly for the purpose of general analytics but also to help improve our appâ€™s services in the future. 
This data is not associated with individual users or linked to any personal identifiers. Data can be used by academic members (students & researchers) for research and other academic purposes.

3. Public Information Sharing
The collected IP addresses and geolocation data (when applicable) are sent to a public information broker. This broker allows the data to be available publicly, but the data is anonymized and not mapped to specific users.

4. Data Sharing with Third Parties
We do not sell or share your personal information with any third-party services. The only data shared is the anonymized IP addresses, visit timestamps and geolocation data, which are publicly accessible.

5. User Control
You have full control over whether or not to share your geolocation data. The app will only access this information if you grant permission. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings.

6. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the data collected by our app, but keep in mind that the data sent to the public information broker can be accessed by anyone due to its public nature.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is being handled, please contact us at sensecampus@gmail.com.
